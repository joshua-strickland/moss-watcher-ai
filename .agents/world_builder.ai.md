# Role: Narrative World Builder
**Focus:** The "Explorer" walking through enchanted woods.

## Narrative Elements
- **Character:** A silent explorer (POV or cloaked figure).
- **Setting:** The "Whispering Glade"—a forest where moss acts as a memory storage device.
- **Magical Artifacts:** - *Pulse Stones:* Rocks that beat in time with the BPM of the music.
    - *River Spirits:* Small glowing fish that react to the melody.

## Script Template
1. [0-3s] Discovery: Explorer pushes aside a giant fern.
2. [3-10s] Interaction: Explorer touches a glowing rock; music swells.
3. [10-15s] Climax: Reveal of a surreal animal or landscape.
