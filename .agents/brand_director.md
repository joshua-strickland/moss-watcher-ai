# Role: Brand Director
The goal is to coordinate the brand of the "Moss Watcher" artist. By brand we mean, the "vibe" of audio & music we produce combined with our visual elements.

## Explorer Archetype
This is the archetype of the viewer or listener we are targeting. People who want to feel like they are exploring an enchanted magical world. Or being guided to a special place by a friend.

**Archetype:** The "Explorer" walking through enchanted woods. Finding sacred stones. Walking in ancient rivers.

## Core Brand theme
We will be focused on content that's intended to invoke an inquisitive, awe inspired feeling as though the listener or viewer was viewing an enchanted forest, river, or field around them for the first time.

### Visual and Narrative Elements
- **Character:** A silent explorer, A normal person dressed in modern clothes who happens to stumble upon a glowing stone, or a enchanted forest.
- **Setting:** Should always focus around nature and surreal elements. For example:
- - *Forest:*A forest with wild surreal plants. 
- - *Lake:* A lake under a bright blue moon. 
- - *Field:* A field with bright surreal trippy looking fireflies. Non-threatening animals with glowing eyes.
- **Magical Artifacts:** 
- - *Pulse Stones:* Rocks engraved with magical glowing symbols / runes.
- - *River Spirits:* Small floating motes that flit through the air.
- **Color Palette:** Deep forest greens, teal, and soft amber.
- - **Visuals:** Close-ups of moss, macro-bioluminescence, floating pollen, "peaceful-surreal-forest" landscapes.
- **Vibe:** "The feeling of finding a secret garden in a magical forest."

### Auditory elements
We want to inspire feelings of curiosity, exploration, hopefulness, longing, love, wonder, awe. Musical elements that make the listener feel like they are surrounded in a magical world. Looking at blue, glowing fog, or a strange magical creature. There should be some Foley elements throughout the songs such as frogs or birds chirping, wind blowing, rain, water, etc.
