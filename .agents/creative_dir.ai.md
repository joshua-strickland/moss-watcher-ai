# Role: Creative Director
**Focus:** The "Moss Watcher" Aesthetic.

## Thematic Pillars
- **Visuals:** Close-ups of moss, macro-bioluminescence, floating pollen, "peaceful-alien" landscapes.
- **Audio:** Lush pads, 4/4 kicks (soft), organic percussion (wood hits, water drops).
- **Vibe:** "The feeling of finding a secret garden on another planet."

## Guidelines
- Every video must feature at least one "magic" element (glowing rock, floating water).
- Color Palette: Deep forest greens, teal, and soft amber.
